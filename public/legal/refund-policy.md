# Refund Policy

**Last Updated: December 8, 2025**

## Overview

This Refund Policy explains our policy regarding refunds for Novakitz premium subscriptions purchased through Gumroad. All refunds are processed through Gumroad's platform and are subject to both our policy and Gumroad's refund terms. Please read this policy carefully before purchasing a subscription.

**Important**: All refund requests must be processed through Gumroad's system. While Gumroad technically allows refunds within 90 days, our policy is to honor refund requests within 30 days of purchase.

## 1. General Policy

### 1.1 Limited Refund Policy
Refunds may be granted within 30 days for **technical issues or billing errors only**.

We do NOT offer refunds for:
- Change of mind after using the service
- Dissatisfaction with AI interpretations
- Normal service usage

### 1.2 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee (No-Usage Only)
We offer a **30-day money-back guarantee** for first-time premium subscribers who have **NOT used the service**. If you have not actively used any premium features, you may request a full refund within 30 days of your initial purchase.

### 1.3 Conditions
The 30-day money-back guarantee applies when:
- This is your first premium subscription purchase
- You request a refund within 30 days of the purchase date
- **You have not actively used the service** (e.g., created dream entries, generated AI interpretations, or used premium features)
- You have not violated our Terms of Service
- You have not engaged in fraudulent or abusive behavior

## 2. Refund Eligibility

### 2.1 Eligible for Refund
You may be eligible for a refund if:
- You request within the 30-day guarantee period (first-time subscribers only)
- There was a technical error that prevented service access
- You were charged incorrectly or charged twice
- The service failed to meet advertised features due to technical issues on our end
- You cancel within 48 hours of purchase (cooling-off period)

### 2.2 NOT Eligible for Refund
Refunds will NOT be provided for:
- **Subscriptions where the service has been actively used** (created dreams, generated AI interpretations, used premium features)
- Renewal subscriptions (only first purchase qualifies for 30-day guarantee)
- Subscriptions cancelled after 30 days from initial purchase
- Partial subscription periods (prorated refunds)
- Change of mind after 30 days
- Change of mind after actively using the service
- Failure to cancel auto-renewal before the next billing cycle
- Violations of Terms of Service
- Account termination due to policy violations

## 3. How to Request a Refund

### 3.1 Refund Request Process
To request a refund:

1. **Email Us**: Send an email to contact@novakitz.shop
2. **Include Information**:
   - Your account email address
   - Purchase date and transaction ID
   - Reason for refund request
   - Any relevant details or screenshots

3. **Response Time**: We aim to respond to refund requests within 2-3 business days

### 3.2 Gumroad Process
**All refunds must be processed through Gumroad**:
- Refunds are issued through Gumroad's dashboard only
- We cannot issue refunds directly through Stripe or PayPal
- Gumroad will return the purchase amount minus the payment processor fee (non-refundable)
- You can also request refunds directly through Gumroad's platform
- Gumroad may issue refunds at their discretion within 90 days to prevent chargebacks

### 3.3 Processing Time
Once approved by Gumroad:
- Refunds are typically processed within 5-10 business days through Gumroad's system
- It may take an additional 3-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your bank or payment provider
- You will receive a confirmation email from Gumroad once the refund is processed
- Payment processor fees (Stripe/PayPal) are non-refundable and retained by the processor

## 4. Subscription Cancellation

### 4.1 Cancelling Your Subscription
You can cancel your subscription at any time:
- **Through Your Account**: Use the subscription management page
- **Through Gumroad**: Manage your subscription directly on Gumroad
- **Email Us**: Contact contact@novakitz.shop

### 4.2 Effect of Cancellation
When you cancel:
- Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period
- You will not be charged for the next billing cycle
- You retain access to premium features until the subscription expires
- No refund is provided for the remaining days of the current period (unless within eligible refund period)

### 4.3 No Auto-Refund
**Important**: Cancelling your subscription does NOT automatically trigger a refund. If you want a refund and are eligible, you must explicitly request it following the process in Section 3.

## 5. Technical Issues and Service Disruptions

### 5.1 Significant Service Outages
If our service experiences significant outages or disruptions:
- We will work to restore service as quickly as possible
- We may provide service credits or subscription extensions
- In cases of prolonged outages (more than 72 consecutive hours), refunds may be considered on a case-by-case basis

### 5.2 Feature Non-Availability
If a core advertised feature becomes unavailable:
- We will notify subscribers via email
- We will work to restore the feature or provide an alternative
- Subscribers may be eligible for refunds if the issue persists beyond 30 days

## 6. Fraudulent or Abusive Behavior

### 6.1 No Refunds for Violations
No refunds will be issued if:
- You violated our Terms of Service
- You engaged in fraudulent activity
- You abused the refund policy (e.g., repeated refund requests across multiple accounts)
- Your account was terminated for policy violations

### 6.2 Fraud Detection
We reserve the right to:
- Investigate refund requests for potential fraud
- Deny refunds if fraud or abuse is detected
- Terminate accounts engaged in fraudulent behavior
- Report fraudulent activity to relevant authorities

## 7. Special Circumstances

### 7.1 Extenuating Circumstances
In rare cases, we may consider refunds outside our standard policy for:
- Medical emergencies or hardships (documentation may be required)
- Death of the account holder (documentation required)
- Natural disasters or extraordinary circumstances

Please contact us at contact@novakitz.shop to discuss your situation.

### 7.2 Case-by-Case Review
Each special circumstance request is reviewed individually. Approval is at our sole discretion and does not set a precedent for future requests.

## 8. Subscription Tiers and Pricing

### 8.1 Different Tiers
Our refund policy applies equally to all subscription tiers:
- Monthly subscriptions
- Annual subscriptions
- Any special promotional pricing

### 8.2 Promotional Offers
For subscriptions purchased at promotional or discounted rates:
- The same refund policy applies
- Refund amount will be the actual amount paid
- Future renewals at standard pricing are not eligible for promotional refunds

## 9. Currency and Payment Methods

### 9.1 Refund Currency
Refunds will be issued in the same currency and to the same payment method used for the original purchase.

### 9.2 Payment Method Changes
If your original payment method is no longer valid:
- Contact us to arrange an alternative refund method
- Additional verification may be required
- Processing time may be extended

### 9.3 Transaction Fees
- **Payment processing fees are non-refundable**: Fees charged by Stripe, PayPal, or other payment processors are retained by those processors
- Gumroad returns their fee minus the payment processor fee
- The refund amount will be the purchase price minus any non-refundable processor fees
- We cannot control or refund fees charged by third-party payment processors

## 10. Free Trial Conversions

### 10.1 Trial to Paid
If you convert from a free trial to a paid subscription:
- The 30-day money-back guarantee starts from the date of your first paid charge
- If you cancel during the trial period (if applicable), no charges occur and no refund is needed

### 10.2 Feature Access
During the refund process, you may lose access to premium features immediately or at the end of the billing period, depending on the situation.

## 11. Geographic Considerations

### 11.1 Consumer Protection Laws
In addition to our refund policy, you may have rights under consumer protection laws in your jurisdiction:
- **EU Citizens**: You have a 14-day cooling-off period under EU law
- **UK Citizens**: You have rights under the Consumer Rights Act
- **Australian Citizens**: You have rights under Australian Consumer Law
- **California Citizens**: You have rights under California Consumer Protection laws

Our policy aims to meet or exceed these requirements, but local laws may provide additional protections.

### 11.2 Exercising Legal Rights
To exercise rights under local consumer protection laws, contact us at contact@novakitz.shop with:
- Your location
- Relevant law or regulation
- Nature of your request

## 12. Data and Content After Refund

### 12.1 Data Retention
After a refund is processed:
- Your account converts to a free plan (if you had one previously)

- Premium features are disabled
- You can delete your account entirely if you wish

### 12.2 Account Deletion
If you want to delete your account after a refund:

## 13. Dispute Resolution

### 13.1 Contact Us First
Before disputing a charge with your bank or credit card company:

# 환불 정책 (한국어)

## 개요

본 환불 정책은 Gumroad를 통해 구매한 Novakitz 프리미엄 구독에 대한 환불 정책을 설명합니다. 모든 환불은 Gumroad 플랫폼을 통해 처리되며 당사 정책과 Gumroad의 환불 약관 모두의 적용을 받습니다. 구독을 구매하기 전에 본 정책을 주의 깊게 읽어주십시오.

**중요**: Gumroad는 차지백을 방지하기 위해 구매 후 90일 이내에 재량으로 환불을 발행할 권리를 보유합니다. 모든 환불 요청은 Gumroad 시스템을 통해 처리되어야 합니다.

## 1. 일반 정책

### 1.1 제한적 환불 정책
환불은 **기술적 문제나 청구 오류의 경우에만** 30일 이내에 승인될 수 있습니다.

다음의 경우 환불을 제공하지 않습니다:
- 서비스 이용 후 마음이 바뀐 경우
- AI 해석에 대한 불만족
- 정상적인 서비스 이용

### 1.2 30일 환불 보장 (미이용자만)
서비스를 **이용하지 않은** 최초 프리미엄 구독자에게 **30일 환불 보장**을 제공합니다. 프리미엄 기능을 실제로 이용하지 않은 경우 최초 구매일로부터 30일 이내에 전액 환불을 요청할 수 있습니다.

### 1.3 조건
다음의 경우 30일 환불 보장이 적용됩니다:
- 최초 프리미엄 구독 구매인 경우
- 구매일로부터 30일 이내에 환불을 요청하는 경우
- **서비스를 실제로 이용하지 않은 경우** (예: 꿈 일기 작성, AI 해석 생성, 프리미엄 기능 사용 등을 하지 않음)
- 이용약관을 위반하지 않은 경우
- 사기 또는 남용 행위에 관여하지 않은 경우

## 2. 환불 자격

### 2.1 환불 가능
다음의 경우 환불 자격이 있을 수 있습니다:
- 30일 보장 기간 내에 요청(최초 구독자만 해당)
- 서비스 접근을 방해한 기술적 오류가 있었던 경우
- 잘못 청구되었거나 이중 청구된 경우
- 당사 측의 기술적 문제로 인해 서비스가 광고된 기능을 충족하지 못한 경우
- 구매 후 48시간 이내에 취소(쿨링오프 기간)

### 2.2 환불 불가
다음의 경우 환불이 제공되지 않습니다:
- **서비스를 실제로 이용한 경우** (꿈 일기 작성, AI 해석 생성, 프리미엄 기능 사용 등)
- 갱신 구독(30일 보장은 최초 구매에만 적용)

- 부분 구독 기간(비례 환불)
- 30일 후 마음이 바뀐 경우
- 서비스를 이용한 후 마음이 바뀐 경우
- 다음 청구 주기 전에 자동 갱신을 취소하지 않은 경우
